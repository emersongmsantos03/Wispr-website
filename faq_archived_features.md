# Archived Features & Platform FAQs
These questions were removed from the live site on 2026-06-08 for future consideration.

---

**Q4: Is there a way to contact other agents inside the platform?**
Yes! Click on the chat icon and type in the agent you would like to connect with.

---

**Q5: Can I create a detailed buyer search that notifies both myself and my client?**
Yes, you have the ability to create more than one buyer search when you enter a buyer's information into the system. Please visit the video tutorial section to walk through this feature step by step.

---

**Q7: Are all of the properties in the Wispr Marketplace listed?**
No. All properties on our marketplace are pre-listed — they are working contacts that agents have in their personal database. The benefit of matching your buyers' needs with one of these contacts is that you can open a legitimate conversation about a deal before anything goes public.

---

**Q8: Should I be concerned about Clear Cooperation?**
Great question. Since these are not actual listings with signed contracts, our legal team has confirmed we are in the clear. Wispr was built to help you move further upstream in the funnel — creating market opportunities for your clients before anything is formally listed.

---

**Q9: Does this data belong to my broker or team lead?**
No. Any data you upload is yours privately. We do not ascribe it to your broker or team lead.

---

**Q10: Are these pocket listings?**
No. These are not listings — they do not have contracts assigned to them. Once a formal listing agreement is signed, you must abide by your local MLS and state rules.

---

**Q14: How do I create multiple searches for one client?**
Go to your Clients tab → find your client → under Actions click the pencil/paper icon → this takes you to the client's profile page → on the left side under Saved Searches, click the + sign and create a new search. If you need additional help, please view one of our tutorials.
