The industry changed.
We built the answer.

The rise of public listing platforms put algorithms between advisors and their clients. Data that once belonged to the advisor — buyer preferences, off-market opportunities, trusted colleague networks — was absorbed by platforms that profited from it.

wispr was built to reverse that. A private, compliant network where advisors share pre-market and off-market inventory with trusted colleagues — not with the algorithm, not with the public, and never with a platform that sells it back to them.
